  ---
  Bank Settings вҖ” Feature Specification

  Feature Overview

  Purpose

  Bank Settings вҖ” LC Issuance Checklist Management enables the Bank Admin to
  configure the verification instruction set used during LC application reviews.
   Bank Admins can add, edit, reorder, toggle, and delete Checklist Items,
  maintaining a tenant-scoped ordered list that drives compliance checks for
  Branch and CTF staff. See FRS #15 вҖ” 1. Purpose
  (http://localhost:8080/root/trade-finance/-/issues/15#1-purpose), FRS #16 вҖ” 1.
   Purpose (http://localhost:8080/root/trade-finance/-/issues/16#1-purpose).

  Scope

  In scope: full CRUD lifecycle for ChecklistItems within a tenant вҖ” listing
  (active and inactive), adding, editing descriptions, toggling active/inactive
  status, reordering one position at a time, and soft-deleting with sequence gap
   closure. Out of scope: the LC application review checklist as seen by Branch
  and CTF staff (separate module), bulk item operations, drag-and-drop
  reordering, and auto-refresh of the configuration screen.

  Business Impact

  Gives Bank Admins direct control over the verification steps presented to
  review staff, ensuring the institution's compliance process is accurately
  reflected in the platform without requiring engineering intervention for
  routine configuration changes.
